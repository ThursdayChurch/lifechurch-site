Leading the Next Generation

At Thursday Church, kids and youth learn the truth about who God is and who they are because of Him. Our goal is to partner with parents to lead the next generation to become fully devoted followers of Christ.

As early as 2 years old, children connect with the Bible and learn about God through age-specific lessons, small groups, and trusted adult leadership.

Konnect

Through their elementary years, kids want to have fun and find a place to belong. Through Konnect, 1st through 4th graders experience a teaching style they can relate to while they connect with other kids and adults invested in their future and faith.

The Loop

Growing from a kid into a teenager can be an awesome time in life. The Loop is designed to help 5th and 6th graders discover who God is and feel confident about who they are in Him.

Roots

Roots is our weekly small group ministry for 5th graders. Each week, kids learn biblical truths, life skills, and spiritual disciplines to apply to help them transition into their middle school years.
