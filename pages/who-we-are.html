Thursday Church wants to make a lasting difference in your life, in our community, and in the world.

Our mission is to lead people to become fully devoted followers of Christ. That's how we're able to make a difference and it's the driving force behind everything we do.

A church isn't a building--it's the people. We meet in locations around the United States and globally online. No matter where you meet us, we think you'll find friendly people that are excited to meet you!

Your life

Wherever you are in life, you matter and you have a purpose. We want to help you become who God has created you to be. Your journey has a next step and we'll help you find it. It could start with serving, finding a God's vision for your life, giving, or joining us regularly .

You don't have to take the next step alone. With a strong community of friends, you can laugh, grow, and serve with people who genuinely care about you. Find a small group—or LIFE Group—to join you on your journey.

Your family

Strong families start with solid foundations. That's why we partner with parents to help raise the next generation to become followers of Christ. Your children and teens have opportunities throughout the week to grow relationships with each other and with God.

Our community and world

We are passionate about serving the global community and demonstrating God's love to others. Whether in person or online, individually or in groups, within our church and around the world, we are focused on supporting and engaging in relationships that provide relief and restoration to a hurting world.

Our leadership

Our Directional Leadership Team works together to shape the vision and direction of Thursday Church.

  • Craig Groeschel
    Senior Pastor

    As the Senior Pastor of Thursday Church, Craig provides leadership and guidance for the church as a whole. Following a vision God gave him for a different kind of church, he and a handful of people launched Thursday Church in 1996.

    He speaks frequently at conferences and has written several books, including his recent release Fight: Winning the Battles that Matter Most. He, his wife Amy, and their six children live in the Edmond, Oklahoma area.

  • Kevin Penry
    Operations Leader

    Kevin leads the overall operations of Thursday Church. With a focus on maintaining the highest level of integrity, he leads the way on church logistics, finances, construction and design, and improving overall efficiency within the organization.

    He also leads our small groups and missions teams as we redefine what ministry and personal development look like in a global community.

  • Bobby Gruenewald
    Innovation Leader

    Bobby is passionate about exploring new ideas and finding practical ways to leverage them for the global Church.

    He oversees the Digital Missions, Media Design, Network Church, and Communications teams.

  • Jerry Hurley
    Team Development Leader

    Jerry is responsible for the team and cultural development at Thursday Church. He directly oversees the Human Resources, Training & Development teams, and Next Gen ministry in addition to helping shape the overall direction and leadership of the church.

  • Sam Roberts
    Campus Operations

    Sam directs the leadership of all campuses and their staff, as well as the overall direction of the church and its initiatives that are implemented at the campus level.
